A category, as used on teoremer, can be seen as a tagÂ with another category as parent. A special root tag is used to represent top-level categories and no cycles are allowed. This way all categories represent a rooted tree, or heirarchy, of tags. Categories are useful for browsing or searching. Each tag may contain spaces and even inline math. For instance,
/ calculus / indefinite integrals / integrals involving [latex]\sqrt{a x + b}[/latex]
is a valid category.
