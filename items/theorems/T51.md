Let $O$ be the center of a circle and let $A$, $B$, $C$ be points on the circle. The
[inscribed angle](#inscribed-angle) $\angle BAC$ equals half the
[central angle](#central-angle) $\angle BOC$ that subtends the same arc $BC$:

$$
\angle BAC = \frac{1}{2} \angle BOC
$$
