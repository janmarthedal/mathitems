The quantity $\binom{n}{k}$ is called a [binomial-coefficient](=binomial-coefficient).

For [real](#real-number) $r$ we have

$$
\binom{r}{k} = \prod_{j=1}^k \frac{r + 1 - j}{j}
$$

for [integer](#integer) $k \geq 0$ and

$$
\binom{r}{k} = 0
$$

for integer $k < 0$.
