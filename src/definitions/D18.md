Let $A$ and $B$ be two [sets](#set) and let $f$ be a [mapping](D14#mapping) of $A$ into $B$.

$f$ is a [1-1 mapping](=one-to-one-mapping) of $A$ into $B$ provided that $f(x_1)\neq f(x_2)$ whenever $x_1\neq x_2$, $x_1\in A$, $x_2\in A$.
